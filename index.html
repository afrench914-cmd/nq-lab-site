Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NQ Lab.

NQ Lab is a trading research group that builds and runs AI-powered models for NQ futures. Each model scans the market in real-time, detects setups based on specific strategies, and delivers alerts to Discord and X. Think of it as a team of trading bots working around the clock.

Each model is an independent trading strategy running its own scanner. The Silver Bullet model detects ICT-based setups during the 10-11 AM kill zone. Other models follow different strategies, timeframes, and rules. Each model has its own bot, alerts channel, and trade tracking.

Every setup is graded from A+ to C based on how many confluence factors align — things like sweeps, displacement strength, HTF bias, key level proximity, and more. A+ means everything lines up. C means fewer factors but still a valid entry. You decide what grade fits your risk tolerance.

No. NQ Lab provides automated detection and analysis — not trade signals or financial advice. The models identify setups and grade them, but every trade decision is yours. We give you the data, you make the call.

NQ Lab is free to join. All alerts, outlooks, and AI assistant access are available in the Discord at no cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.

Right now we focus exclusively on NQ (Nasdaq 100 E-mini futures) and MNQ (Micro). NQ has the volatility and liquidity that our models thrive on. We may expand to other instruments as new models are developed.

Absolutely. We're always testing new edges. Drop your idea in the Discord and if it has merit, we'll build and backtest it. Some of our best models started as community suggestions.
